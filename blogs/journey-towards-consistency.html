My Journey towards consistency

February 25, 2024

I am a person who is living his normal life like any 23 year old do. Waking up, doing same routines all over the week days and waiting for the weekend. And in weekend doing nothing, I will just sleep and have a very lazy day. But my dream life is not this. I want to do something meaningful in my life. I am constantly feeling like I am not living up to my potential. I can be more in life. As a web series fan I would like to quote I want to be like Mike Ross or Harvey specter from Suits or Bobby Axelrod from Billions. But what I'm actually doing is getting me nothing like them.

I tried to focus and do things that matter and do stuffs that matter. But what I lack is action. Because every day as I recall my productive time is of average an hour or two (Other than my work timings). On other times I always spending time unproductive or thinking/watching random things. Or searching for motivation and as I get that motivation I again search for more. I don't execute things.

Why am I doing this?

My mind never aligns for the things I want. It always feels comfortable when watching any motivation videos or any videos that tells somethings or web series or movies from Netflix. I will start doing things that matters but I actually never done it consistently. I have been on the same cycle for long. I severely lack consistency.

What do I want to Achieve?

I strongly believe in the quote that "Mind is the ultimate tool that humans possess". So I want to improve myself and bring consistency & good Mental Stability.

The Things that I want to do consistently.

  • Hit the Gym. (Being in a good shape matters to me).
  • Drink atleast 2 litres of water in a day.
  • Do typing practice. (My current speed in 32 WPM. I want to achieve > 50wpm).
  • Read atleast 5 pages daily. (I want to complete atleast 3 books in this year).

Other than that doing the daily planned tasks or work tasks.

Progress Tracking

I've created a consistency graph, which shows am I being consistent or not. Also I tweet about my progress on Twitter.

Takeaway

I'm Doing this for improving myself and if you too want to do something like this. Or you just want someone to be accountable for we can connect and grow together.
